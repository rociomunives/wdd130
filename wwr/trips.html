Dry Oar Trips

Wild Adventure

Difficulty Level: Advanced
Duration: 1 day
Description: Prepare yourself for the ultimate adrenaline rush with our Wild Adventure rafting trip! Navigate through raging rapids and conquer challenging whitewater sections of the river. Our expert guides will lead you through thrilling twists and turns as you experience the power of nature up close. This full-day adventure promises non-stop excitement and breathtaking views that will leave you exhilarated and wanting more.

Natural Exploration

Difficulty Level: Intermediate
Duration: 2 days
Description:Embark on a journey of discovery with our Natural Exploration rafting trip! Immerse yourself in the stunning beauty of untouched wilderness as you navigate through calm stretches and exhilarating rapids. Over two days, you'll have the chance to explore hidden coves, lush forests, and breathtaking landscapes. Our knowledgeable guides will share fascinating insights about the local flora and fauna, making this trip not only thrilling but also educational and enriching.

Thrilling Rapids

Difficulty Level: Beginner
Duration: Half day
Description: Looking for a quick adrenaline fix? Our Thrilling Rapids rafting trip is perfect for beginners and thrill-seekers alike! Spend a half-day navigating through exciting rapids and enjoying the rush of cool water splashing around you. Our experienced guides will ensure your safety while providing tips and tricks to enhance your rafting experience. Whether you're new to rafting or a seasoned adventurer, this trip guarantees an unforgettable adventure filled with fun and excitement.

Family-Friendly Expedition

Difficulty Level:Beginner
Duration: Full day
Description: Get ready for a family adventure like no other with our Family-Friendly Expedition rafting trip! This full-day excursion is specially designed to provide fun and excitement for adventurers of all ages. Experience the thrill of navigating gentle rapids and tranquil waters as you journey down the river together. Our experienced guides will ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone, with plenty of opportunities for swimming, water games, and riverside picnics. Create unforgettable memories with your loved ones as you bond over shared experiences and immerse yourselves in the beauty of nature.
