Dry Oar

Our mission is to connect people with nature through exciting aquatic adventures, promoting conservation and responsible enjoyment of the environment. We believe in the power of teamwork, overcoming challenges and building meaningful connections in a natural setting.
"Paddle, laugh, live!" - Our invitation to fully enjoy the moment.

History

Born in the mountains of Potrerillos, Mendoza, Argentina, our rafting company was formed over a decade ago by a group of friends passionate about water and adventure. Since then, we've offered thrilling rafting experiences on the rapids of the Mendoza River, committed to conserving the natural environment. Rooted in Potrerillos, we've been a driving force for local employment and community development, while supporting conservation initiatives and promoting sustainable practices. Potrerillos is not just our home but also the source of our inspiration and commitment to environmental preservation.
