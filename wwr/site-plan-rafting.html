Overview

Purpose

The purpose of Dry Oar rafting is to provide exciting and safe whitewater rafting experiences for clients of all ages and skill levels. The purpose of the website is to provide comprehensive and engaging information to attract potential customers, offer an online experience that reflects the thrill and adventure of rafting, provide information and examples of different adventures or trips the company offers including equipment availability, and provide company information.

Audience

The target audience for the rafting website are adventurers, outdoor enthusiasts, travelers and tourists, families and groups, and corporate groups.
For example:
Adventurers. 25-35 years old. Middle-income with disposable income for leisure activities.Interested on seek adventure and unique experiences, often involved in outdoor activities, travel, and socializing. Young professionals, possibly without children. They want a memorable adventure with opportunities for outdoor exploration, physical activity, and social experiences.On the website, they want to find clear information on rafting tours, engaging visuals showcasing the excitement of rafting, and reviews from individuals who have enjoyed the experience.
Corporate groups. 30-50 years old. Middle to upper-income, budgeting for team-building activities. Interested in activities fostering teamwork, leadership, and communication skills. Business professionals, managers, and team leaders looking for unique team-building experiences. They want activities that are engaging and promote collaboration, a break from routine work environments, memorable working experiences. On the website, they want to see corporate packages for team-building events, information on how rafting promotes teamwork and leadership, and testimonials from companies that have utilized the services.
Families groups. 30-45 years old. Middle-income families with a budget for vacations. Interested on outdoor activities, creating lasting memories. Families with children, looking for inclusive and exciting activities. They want family-friendly adventure experiences, and safety assurance for children.On website, they expect to find details on family-friendly rafting trips, safety measures and guidelines for children, and options for family packages.
